Scholarship

We’ve helped hundereds of international students reach their study goals

We know that tertiary study in Australia can be expensive, but many institutions recognise this and provide scholarships to honour your commitment to your studies.These scholarships are specifically designed to ease some of the burden for high-achieving and disadvantaged students, so you can concentrate on achieving the best possible results without being concerned about money.

  • Aid is available in some form for many study areas and institutions
  • We’ll help you easily find and apply for scholarships
  • You could save tens of thousands of dollars off your course fees

    Health Cover

    INSURERS

    Australian Unity

    Australian Unity has been supporting the well-being of Australians since 1840. They offer insurance plans for both working and non-working visas. Some of the services and treatments that are covered include:

    • Doctor and specialist fees,
    • Emergency ambulance,
    • Hospital treatment and accommodation,
    • Day surgery and other procedures

    Iman

    Iman provides health cover for graduate 485 visa, TSS 482 visa, Temporary (skilled) 457 visa and other working types visas. Some of the major offerings include:

    • Offers short-term health insurance to temporary working visitors
    • Gives access to the subsidised Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (PBS)
    • Coverage for outpatient- prescribed pharmaceuticals
    • Takes care of all medical expenses related to in–hospital (In–Patient) care

    Allianz

    Allianz Global Assistance provides access to a network of insurance providers to help you pay your medical bills. They also have representatives on campuses around Australia to help in person. Some of the services and treatments that are covered include:

    • Doctor’s appointments,
    • Specialists(out of hospital),
    • Prescription medicine,
    • Hospital cost,
    • Emergency ambulance,
    • Medical tests

    Bupa

    Bupa is a healthcare company committed to helping customers avail the most affordable insurance plans. They offer a broad range of services, including aged care and retirement, dental, optical, health insurance, and community wellbeing initiatives, to improve the health of all Australians. For overseas visitors, their major offerings are:

    • Coverage for both inpatient and outpatient services,
    • Receive support when you need it with retail centres in key locations,
    • Access a range of health discounts, tools and more,
    • Exclusive Bupa 24 hour advice line for phone-based support

    medibank

    Medibank is a leading private health insurer, providing health insurance through their Medibank and ahm brands as well as a range of health services across Australia. Whether an English language course or a university degree, Medibank's Overseas Student Health Covers (OSHC) meet student visa health insurance requirements. Some of the services they offer include:

    • Accident and emergency department facility fees,
    • Inpatient medical services,
    • General Practitioner (GP) consultations,
    • Other out-of-hospital medical services (blood tests and X-rays)

    NIB

    NIB is one of Australia’s largest health insurers , covering more than one million people in Australia and New Zealand. It provides access to a community of doctors and health specialists to meet international students' medical needs and meet Australian government’s international student visa requirements. Some of its major offerings include:

    • Provides access to trusted health professionals,
    • Provides access to a 24/7 medical support across Australia,
    • Agreements with Australia’s renowned private hospital to help you save money,
    • Process access to a member discount program valid for the entire duration of your policy

    AHM

    AHM is a trusted health insurance provider, serving more than 700,000 Australians over a period of 40 years. Backed by Australia’s largest health insurer, Medibank, it takes pride in providing affordable and flexible health insurance, a seamless online experience and top-notch digital first service. Some of their major offerings include:

    • Health Fund is a not-for-profit,
    • Fast & Simple Claims,
    • Affordable costs from accidents to selected surgeries

    CBHS

    For more than 70 years, CBHS has offered high quality policies, reserved only for current and former CBA Group employees, contractors and their families:

    • Easy claims system,
    • No extra charge for claims on ambulance services,
    • Access to over 7,500 dental and optical providers who provide exceptional treatment while reducing or removing the gap
