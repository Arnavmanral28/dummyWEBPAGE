Why study Automotive in Australia?

The demand for skilled technicians in the automotive industry is on the rise in the last five years and the future looks promising. Automotive courses are the right choice for the ones who want to get their hands on electrical and mechanical systems and gain expertise in their end to end functioning. An automotive technician makes $1450 per week on average and there are multiple visa options available for skilled candidates. For individuals with technical skills and experience, this industry also opens up self-employment opportunities.

Pathway to PR for Automotive degree in Australia

As these skills consistently feature in the MLTSSL, studying these courses is a wise decision if you are aspiring for PR.

Complete a certificate/diploma course in Automotive technology
Get your skills assessed positively by Trades Recognition Australia (TRA)
Lodge your EOI at once you acquire sufficient PR points and/or meet the state requirements
Get invited to apply for one of the permanent visa options
